Data-driven decision support in public health and clinical care

Mission

Ukubona builds decision-support tools that translate statistical models into intuitive visual insights for patients, clinicians, researchers, and students. Our platform integrates curated datasets, multivariable regression outputs, and real-time visualization tools to support informed, personalized choices.

Core Features

  • Modular Platform: Ingests CSV files with beta coefficients and variance-covariance matrices, outputs interactive Kaplan-Meier–style survival curves and 95% confidence intervals.
  • Clinical Use Case: Personalized risk visualization for living kidney donors, with a focus on older adults.
  • Extensible Architecture: Dataset-agnostic design enables rapid deployment of new risk models across healthcare, research domains, and educational domains.

Technology Stack

  • Back-End: Python-based statistical engine using Cox regression
  • Front-End: JavaScript and HTML5 for interactive graphing
  • Hosting: GitHub Pages with embedded dynamic content

About Ukubona

Ukubona is a data science and public health company focused on bridging the gap between population-level statistics and individual-level decisions. We prioritize transparency, interactivity, and collaboration in all our tools.
